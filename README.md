Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to help HR teams analyze workforce data, monitor employee attrition, and understand workforce demographics. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, enabling data-driven decision-making for employee retention and workforce planning.

---

🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall workforce and employee attrition.
- Monitor key HR metrics using interactive KPI cards.
- Identify departments and job roles with the highest attrition.
- Analyze employee distribution by age, gender, salary, and experience.
- Support HR professionals in making data-driven retention strategies.

---

📂 Dataset

The dataset contains HR employee records, including:

- Employee ID
- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Age
- Salary Slab
- Years of Experience
- Job Satisfaction
- Attrition Status (Active/Exited)

Total Employees: 1,365

Interactive Visualizations

- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Salary Slab Distribution
- Job Role vs Job Satisfaction Matrix
- Age Group Analysis
- Gender Distribution
- Experience Distribution
- Department Funnel Chart
- Department Slicer for Dynamic Filtering

---

🔍 Key Insights

- Operations and Administration departments have the highest employee attrition.
- Employees earning between 6–10 LPA show the highest attrition.
- Employees with 0–5 years of experience are more likely to leave.
- The majority of employees belong to the 26–45 years age group.
- Male employees represent approximately 60% of the workforce, while female employees account for 40%.
